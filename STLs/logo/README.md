### Logo MMU
Printed as a single multicolor part and requires a printer equipped with an MMU system.
### Logo MMU 3-Part
A split version of the MMU logo designed for printers with smaller build volumes. Printed as three multicolor sections and assembled after printing. Requires an MMU system.

### Slicer MMU Logo
To print the Multi Color logo, adjust your slicer settings and enable the "Interface Shells" option under the Multimaterial section.

This setting helps improve the interface quality between different colors, resulting in a cleaner and more consistent finish.

### Logo Multi Part
Printed as separate colored parts that are assembled after printing. Does not require an MMU system.
### Logo Multi Part 3-Part
A split version of the Multi-Part logo designed for printers with smaller build volumes. Printed as three separate sections and assembled after printing. Does not require an MMU system!
